The Perfect PB & J

Description

This Peanut Butter and Jelly is my favoritte sandwhich.It has the perfect balance of inrgedients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Whitebread
  • 1 jar of Grape Jelly
  • 1 jar if creamy Peanut Butter
  • A butter Knife
  • A sharp Knife
  • A Cutting Board

Directions

  1. Lay Both slices of bread next to each other on the cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife. Spread 1/8 inches of peanut butter on the left bread.
  3. On the right bread, spread 1/8 of the jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two breads together so that both the peanut butter and the jelly is touching.
  5. With the sharp knife carefully cut the sandwich in half.
  6. Enjoy that sandwich.
